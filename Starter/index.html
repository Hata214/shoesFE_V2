Shoe Care Guide

Cleaning

  • Use a soft brush to remove loose dirt
  • Clean with mild soap and warm water
  • Air dry at room temperature

Protection

  • Apply water repellent spray
  • Use shoe trees when storing
  • Rotate shoes regularly

Maintenance

  • Replace insoles when worn
  • Check soles for wear patterns
  • Polish leather shoes monthly
